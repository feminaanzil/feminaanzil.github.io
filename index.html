About Me

Research meets real-world operations

I am a data science professional and MSc student at Emirates Aviation University, UAE, where my research focuses on applying big data and machine learning to solve real-world challenges in aviation safety, visibility prediction, and environmental sustainability.

With extensive experience in project management as PMO Manager at United Al Saqer Group, I bring a unique combination of operational leadership and cutting-edge AI research to every project I undertake.

My work spans multi-hazard climate risk intelligence, atmospheric visibility forecasting, air quality analytics, and real estate prediction — all built on rigorous data pipelines and explainable AI frameworks.
